About Us

Hello! I'm a young and passionate entrepreneur with a deep love for all things tech. Ever since I was a kid, I've been fascinated by how devices work — whether it was taking apart old phones, upgrading laptops, or helping friends fix their consoles.

Starting NorthTech Repairs is more than just a business venture — it's a dream come true. I’m ecstatic to finally turn my passion into a purpose by helping people get their tech back in top shape. I believe in reliable repairs, honest service, and building a community of customers who feel taken care of.

Whether it's a cracked screen, slow laptop, or something you just can’t figure out, NorthTech Repairs is here to help — with enthusiasm, skill, and a smile!
